# Executive Engineering Leadership & Offer Negotiation

## The Path to Leadership
Platform engineering leadership involves guiding entire organizations toward better DevOps and platform practices, establishing standards, and ensuring developer productivity.

## Offer Negotiation Strategies
1. **Never accept the first offer immediately:** Take time to review the entire compensation package.
2. **Understand the compensation structure:** Base salary, RSU/Equity, and Sign-on bonuses.
3. **Communicate value:** Remind them of the specific problems you will solve for their infrastructure.
