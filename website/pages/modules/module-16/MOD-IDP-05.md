# Measuring Developer Experience (DevEx) & Platform Adoption

A platform is only successful if it is adopted and improves the lives of developers.

## DORA Metrics

DORA metrics are essential for understanding software delivery performance:
- Deployment Frequency
- Lead Time for Changes
- Time to Restore Service
- Change Failure Rate

## SPACE Framework

The SPACE framework provides a holistic view of developer productivity:
- Satisfaction and well-being
- Performance
- Activity
- Communication and collaboration
- Efficiency and flow

By combining qualitative surveys with quantitative metrics, platform teams can continuously iterate and improve the platform.
