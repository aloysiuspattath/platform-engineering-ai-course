# Incident Command Management & On-Call Workflows

Incidents are inevitable. How you handle them determines the impact on your customers.

## Incident Command System (ICS)
ICS is a standardized approach to the command, control, and coordination of emergency response.

## Roles in an Incident
1. **Incident Commander (IC):** Holds high-level state, assigns tasks, and drives the incident to resolution.
2. **Operations / Subject Matter Expert:** Executes tasks to investigate and mitigate the incident.
3. **Communications Lead:** Communicates internally and externally about the incident status.

## On-Call Workflows
Managing alert fatigue, scheduling hand-offs, and ensuring actionable alerts are routed appropriately.
