# High-Impact Portfolio Refinement & Industry Presentation

## Why a Portfolio Matters
In platform engineering, talk is cheap. A GitHub repository showing a fully automated Kubernetes deployment via Terraform and ArgoCD proves you can do the job.

## Building Your Portfolio
1. **Highlight the Architecture:** Provide diagrams (e.g., Mermaid, Excalidraw) for your projects.
2. **Document Decisions:** Include Architectural Decision Records (ADRs).
3. **Showcase the CI/CD:** Make sure your GitHub Actions workflows are clean and visible.

## Presenting Your Work
When walking a recruiter or hiring manager through your portfolio, focus on the business value of what you built. Did you reduce deployment time? Did you improve reliability?
