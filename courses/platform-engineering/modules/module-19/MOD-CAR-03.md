# Behavioral Engineering Interviews & Engineering Communication

## The STAR Method
When answering behavioral questions, use the STAR format:
- **Situation:** Set the scene and give the necessary details of your example.
- **Task:** Describe what your responsibility was in that situation.
- **Action:** Explain exactly what steps you took to address it.
- **Result:** Share what outcomes your actions achieved.

## Common Behavioral Topics
1. Tell me about a time you broke production.
2. How do you handle disagreements with a senior engineer?
3. Describe a time you advocated for a technical change that was initially rejected.

## Engineering Communication
As a platform engineer, you serve other developers. Your communication must be clear, concise, and empathetic to their challenges.
